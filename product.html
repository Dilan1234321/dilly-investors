The Product

Four surfaces. One profile. Zero scores.

Everything Dilly does is grounded in a living profile the user builds through conversation. No Smart/Grit/Build. No ranking. No leaderboards. Fit narratives, letters, disruption intelligence. Every surface is a read on the profile, not a reduction of it.

01 · Foundation

The Dilly Profile

A structured, growing record of who the user is: experiences, goals, fears, beyond-resume facts, target companies, deadlines. Built from conversation, seeded from a resume, edited directly. 16+ fact categories stored as structured JSON with dual-write to Postgres. The profile is the product. Everything else is a read on it.

02 · Signal

Fit Narratives

For every job, Dilly writes a personal read: what the user has that matches, what's missing, what to do. One powerful sentence that never truncates across devices. Haiku 4.5. ~$0.0002 per narrative. Cached per user-job pair. Pre-generated for the entire feed via batch runs, not on-demand.

Chapter

A weekly coaching brief written by Dilly that reads the full profile, connects dots across experiences, and delivers one concrete move. Not generic advice. A specific, actionable call-to-action grounded in what Dilly knows about this user, their goals, and where they are stuck. A structural reason to return every week.

Three Modes, One Codebase

The product reshapes around where the user is.

Every competitor is a single-surface tool. Dilly captures users across life events, so there is no reason to leave the system when career status changes. The switch itself is a retention event.

Mode 01

Student

Pre-first-job

Before the first full-time role. Internship hunt, ATS-tailored resumes, tutorials, audits. Built for .edu. The profile grows through onboarding, voice chats, and resume upload.

  • Internship feed, cohort-matched
  • ATS resume builder
  • Parent-invite + family plans
  • Interview prep + mock rounds
Mode 02

Seeker

Actively applying

Between jobs. Any email accepted (the general path). Job feed with fit narratives, tailored resumes per role, pipeline tracker, recruiter outreach templates, and interview practice that reads your actual profile.

  • Per-job fit narrative
  • Application tracker + Kanban
  • Recruiter outreach via Resend
  • Apply-Through-Dilly verified badge
Mode 03

Holder

Employed · benchmarking

Already in the job. The My Career dashboard: comp benchmark, trajectory timeline, skills arsenal, tenure read, raise brief, and an always-ready escape hatch. Career management for the 90% of time you're not applying.

  • Compensation benchmark
  • Skills arsenal + trajectory
  • Raise brief generator
  • One-tap escape hatch → Seeker
Users flip between modes via a life-event switch in Settings. "I got a job" / "I lost my job." Each flip triggers a mode-specific transition tutorial. The profile and history carry over; the product changes.
Retention Architecture

The moat isn't the features. It's the return.

Career tools are graveyards. Users sign up once and forget. Dilly ships retention primitives as first-class product, engineered for daily and weekly return on a surface that otherwise sees a user twice a year.

Daily

Home Feed Cards

A rotating daily card: Mon weekly brief, Tue AI threat check, Wed profile gap nudge, Thu job digest, Fri win-of-the-week. One-tap engagement. Built to return, not to demand.

Weekly

Cohort Pulse

Weekly precomputed card: metrics, trends, peer comparisons, scoped to the user's cohort. Batch-generated via Haiku at ~$0.0003 per pulse.

Memory

Second Brain

Chronological career timeline: events, decisions, achievements, connections. A log of what you did, not what you failed at. Worth opening the app just to add to.

Intelligence

AI Arena · Weekly Signal

A weekly curated signal about the user's field, framed through 18 path-aware advisor lenses. Same data, 18 different readings. A structural reason to return when no one else in the category offers one.
