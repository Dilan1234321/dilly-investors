The full product

Every feature below is built and running. Not wireframes. Not mockups. Production code scoring real student resumes.

Core Feature

Resume Audit & Dilly Score

Upload a resume. Get scored across Smart (academic rigor), Grit (leadership & impact), and Build (track-specific proof). Each dimension is 0-100, weighted by career track. Evidence cited from the student's actual content.

SmartGritBuild
"Your Grit score is held back by vague verbs. Change 'assisted with' to 'supported 200+ visits/week' — quantify impact."
AI Coaching

Dilly AI — Career Coach

Conversational AI that knows the student's resume, scores, applications, deadlines, and career track. Students ask "Am I ready for Stripe?" and get a real analysis based on their actual gaps. Coach mode and practice mode for mock interviews.

Full context awareness24/7 availability
"Your Build gap is the real problem — 16 points below Stripe's bar. Ship one project in 4-6 weeks and you're competitive."
Job Intelligence

Internship Matching & Gap Analysis

97 real internships from companies like Stripe, Airbnb, Cloudflare. Each listing shows readiness — Ready, Almost, or Gap — with Smart/Grit/Build bars. A personalized insight tells the student exactly how to close their gaps.

ReadyAlmostGap
ATS Intelligence

ATS Scanner — 6 Systems

Scans the resume against Greenhouse, Workday, iCIMS, Taleo, Lever, and SuccessFactors. Per-system scores, what the ATS extracts, formatting issues, and specific fixes. Company lookup tells students which ATS their target company uses.

Resume Editor with Live Scoring

Every bullet point gets a score. Students see "Good" vs "Needs work" in real time. AI suggests specific rewrites with quantified impact. Overall score updates as they edit.
