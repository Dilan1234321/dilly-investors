The Moat
Five compounding advantages.

1. Trusted Score Signal

The dilly score becomes the shared language between students, universities, and employers. Once a score is trusted, switching costs are enormous, like switching away from credit scores.

2. Continuous Data Improvement

Every audit, every completion, every hiring outcome makes the scoring model smarter. More students = better scores = more trust = more students. Data flywheel that competitors can't replicate without the user base.

3. Habit Formation

Scoring, coaching, learning, job search, and career planning all live in one place. Students check dilly daily. The integrated system creates switching costs that no single-feature competitor can match.

4. Lower-Cost, Higher-Signal Recruiting

Recruiters get pre-scored, .edu-verified candidates, eliminating the 60% of applications that are junk. Once employers trust the signal, they recruit through dilly first.

5. Measurable Outcomes for Universities

Career centers get real data on student readiness for the first time. dilly becomes the tech layer that makes career services measurable, accountable, and scalable.
