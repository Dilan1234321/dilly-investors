The Vision

Four businesses. One profile. Unlimited surface area.

Dilly today is the consumer app: Student, Seeker, Holder. That's the wedge. The vision fans out in four directions: consumer verticals, a B2B platform, an AI auto-apply agent that uses the full power of the profile, and a long-term precision staffing operation that runs at margins no traditional agency can touch. Every branch reuses the same engine. The profile is always the product.

The Long Game

Vela. An AI staffing operation that runs at margins no agency can touch.

Traditional staffing is a $650B industry built on phone calls and gut instinct. Agencies charge 15–25% of first-year compensation per placement, and they run on hundreds of human recruiters manually matching resumes to job descriptions. It's the most obvious industry in the world to disrupt and nobody has done it because nobody had the profile data to replace the human judgment.

Dilly has the profiles. Dilly Agent proves the auto-placement capability. Vela is what happens when you combine them at scale: an AI-run staffing operation that places candidates with employer-grade precision, takes the same fee, and runs with a fraction of the overhead.

Vela by Dilly · AI Staffing
Horizon · 2028+ Horizon

How Vela makes money

Standard staffing take rate: 15–20% of first-year compensation per successful placement. At $70K average starting salary for a student placement, that's $10,500–$14,000 per hire. A traditional agency places 200 candidates a year per recruiter at high overhead. Vela places at scale with AI doing the matching, the tailoring, and the submission.

Alternate model: employer subscription for exclusive early access to the Dilly candidate pool, plus placement fees on conversions. Combines the recurring revenue of B2B SaaS with the transaction revenue of a staffing agency.

→ Why now: Dilly Agent builds the capability. The profile database builds the supply. Vela is the business model that monetizes both at the margin structure no headcount-heavy agency can compete with.
Structural Advantage Horizon

Why nobody else can build this

The hard part of a staffing agency isn't the business model. It's the candidate intelligence. Agencies spend most of their time building a picture of who the candidate actually is what they want, what they've done, what they're afraid of, what they'll actually accept.

Dilly already has that. Every profile has 16+ fact categories, a structured record of goals and fears, a history of every job application, and a calibrated fit model. By the time a candidate enters the Vela placement pipeline, a traditional recruiter would need six phone calls to get the same picture. Vela has it on day one.

→ The moat: the profile is the barrier to entry. Anyone can launch a staffing agency. Nobody else can launch one with this quality of candidate data at this scale.
